A New Way to Bet

The AI powered betting application to take the casinos out the game. Bet against friends or the greater community and remove the risk of a full loss to the bookie. By removing the third party element, it keeps the money recycled and reinvested back into future bets within the community, fostering an envirnment that is more about the social outcomes and competition of the games, rather than purely focused towards money.

No Fees

No fees on transactions whether purchased, or bet. You should be able to bet with your friends without worrying about extra fees. Your win, is your win and we take no part.

AI Functionality

Introducing our AI assistant, built to provide transaction assistance, bet facilitation, bracket creation, impartial league commissioner, payment collector, friend finder and many more.

Be the Best!

Social betting takes the stand here, with league tables, rewards, trash talk as well as location functionality to find and curate new friendships and community centered around the drive of competition.
